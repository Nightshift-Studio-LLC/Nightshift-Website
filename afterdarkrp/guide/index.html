Quick Start

Player Basics

A practical start for AfterDark light RP: choose a job, make money, take risks, and let heat, fines, arrests, and prison resolve trouble.

Getting Started

  • Join as Citizen by default.
  • Press Tab to open the Player Menu, pick a job, and see what that role is built to do.
  • Press B to open Pockets and manage carried items.
  • Watch the lower-left HUD for your current job and money.
  • Start legal for steady cash, or take a criminal job when you want heat, chase, and risk.

Core Controls

General

  • E Use / interact
  • Shift Run modifier
  • G Drop
  • V Voice
  • Enter Chat

Menu + UI

  • Tab Toggle Player Menu
  • B Toggle Pockets
  • Esc Close ATM/Crime panel
  • 1-0 Inventory slot keys

Money and Banking

  • Use ATM with E while looking at an ATM.
  • Deposit pocket cash when you want it safer; withdraw when you need cash on hand.
  • Bank money is safer. Pocket cash is useful, visible in the loop, and can be lost.
  • On death, pocket cash can become a nearby money bag pickup.

Crime Terminal

  • Use E on a crime terminal to open records.
  • Check wanted level, active charges, and outstanding fines.
  • Pay fines when you want to cool things down, or stay hot and deal with police pressure.

Heat and Consequences

  • Trouble moves through systems: wanted state, heat, fines, police pressure, arrest, and prison.
  • If you are wanted, expect pursuit. Hide, run, pay fines, or get caught.
  • If you are police, use the tools and statuses instead of turning crime into a rule call.
  • Criminal play is expected. Exploits, farming, harassment, and broken-state abuse are not.

AfterDark is light RP: messy city stories, system consequences, minimal courtroom energy.
