Player Guide

Guide

Quick start for the current AfterDarkRP implementation, based on the live systems in the project folder.

Getting Started

  • Join as Citizen by default.
  • Open Player Menu with Tab to switch jobs and review salary paths.
  • Open Pockets with B for your slot inventory view (20-slot layout).
  • Track cash/job from HUD in the lower-left corner.

Core Controls

General

  • E Use / interact
  • Shift Run modifier
  • G Drop
  • V Voice
  • Enter Chat

Menu + UI

  • Tab Toggle Player Menu
  • B Toggle Pockets
  • Esc Close ATM/Crime panel
  • 1-0 Inventory slot keys

Money and Banking

  • Use ATM with E while looking at an ATM.
  • Withdraw/deposit amounts from the ATM panel presets or typed value.
  • Bank is persistent; pocket cash can be dropped or lost on death.
  • On death, current pocket cash becomes a nearby money bag pickup.

Crime Terminal

  • Use E on a crime terminal to open records.
  • View wanted level, active charges, and outstanding fines.
  • Pay custom amount or use Pay All Fines directly from the panel.

Police Workflow (Current Build)

  • Mouse1 applies police taze while aiming at a target player.
  • E arrests a stunned target.
  • 1 resolves detainee as charge/release.
  • 2 sends detainee to prison with sentence from charge count.

These are current interaction bindings from `NSDarkRPStatusInteractionSystem` and may change as weapon/loadout integration is finalized.
