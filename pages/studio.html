Studio Values

How the studio evaluates work

  • Systems that create emergent stories instead of scripted answers.
  • Worlds that feel lived in, layered, and slightly dangerous.
  • Progression that rewards mastery without flattening tension.

Operations

How Nightshift works

  • Prototype quickly and cut early when a loop is not proving itself.
  • Treat lighting, materials, sound, and motion as one system.
  • Use public-facing devlogs to keep current work visible and legible.
